How to bake a delicious brownie

What do we need?

  • Chocolate
  • 2eggs
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Step by Step

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. let's chop the chocolate bars and melt it smoothly.

  3. Cut up the butter and add in to the chocolate. Heat and stir over low heat
    until all of the chocolate and butter is melted together.

  4. Add the sugar to the melted chocolate and butter. Stir with a wooden spoon until the sugar is completely mixed into the chocolate and butter.

  5. Fifth, Add the eggs one at a time. Stir until the egg is completely incorporated into the chocolate mixture.

  6. Sixth, Add 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.

  7. Seventh, Add flour. In a separate bowl stir together the flour and baking soda. Add the flour mixture all at once to the chocolate mixture.

  8. Eighth, Gently stir the brownie batter until the flour is moistened.If you want you can add some chocolate pieces or nuts.

  9. Ninth, Bake brownies at 350 degrees F for 25 to 35 minutes.

  10. Eat and enjoy!
